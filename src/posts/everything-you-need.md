## Headings

Markdown supports six levels of headings:

# Heading Level 1
## Heading Level 2
### Heading Level 3
#### Heading Level 4
##### Heading Level 5
###### Heading Level 6

Headings should scale well, respect hierarchy, and remain readable on all screen sizes.

## Paragraphs & Text Styling

Markdown paragraphs are simple, but powerful.

You can emphasize text using **bold**, *italic*, or ***both***.  
You can also use ~~strikethrough~~ when needed.
